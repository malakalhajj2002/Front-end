Our Story

It all began in 2018, back when I was decorating my own
home, thats where I discovered my passion for home décor
products. So I started my own online store, it had begun
with small steps and it is now spreading beautifully to every
home!



Our Goal

My goal is to make every home cozy and happy with my products, every item is carefully selected to be able to meet my client’s needs!



The Beginning

I started Mimdeco in November 2018. When I first started, I
didn’t have much knowledge about the online industry. I
selected items that I would love to have at my own house, so
that if they weren't sold, I could keep them.

Only one week after, I got my first client ! I still remember how
excited I was seeing how it kicked off perfectly, better than I
have imagined it would.

I would love to thank you for purchasing Mimdeco's products,
for your loyalty, your encouragement and your trust.
